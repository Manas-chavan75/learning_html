Information About Chairs

A chair is a piece of furniture designed for one person to sit on, typically featuring a seat, backrest, and legs. Chairs are incredibly versatile and come in a vast array of styles, materials, and functionalities, from basic stools to elaborate ergonomic designs. Chairs have a rich history, evolving from status symbols to commonplace items used in homes, offices, and various other settings.
